Frequently Asked Questions

Is telemedicine right for all medical conditions?

While telemedicine is suitable for many conditions, some situations may require in-person visits. Our doctors will advise if an in-person examination is necessary.

Can I get prescriptions through telemedicine?

Yes, our doctors can prescribe many medications as appropriate. However, certain controlled substances may require in-person visits.

How much does a consultation cost?

Consultation fees vary by service type. Many insurance plans cover telemedicine visits. Check with your insurance provider for coverage details.
